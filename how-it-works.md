### Start Here

Every client journey begins with a focused conversation about where you are, what is getting in the way, and what you want to change. From there, we build a practical plan that fits your goals, schedule, and real life.

### The Process

#### Step 1: Discovery Call

A focused first conversation to understand your goals, challenges, and priorities.

- Review what is working and what is not
- Identify your desired outcomes
- Determine fit for coaching, consulting, or a combined approach

Length: 20 minutes
Fee: Complimentary

#### Step 2: Clarity and Strategy

You receive a clear roadmap with practical next steps.

- Define goals and success markers
- Identify barriers, patterns, and leverage points
- Prioritize actions for immediate progress

#### Step 3: Ongoing Sessions and Implementation

This is where change happens. Sessions are structured, supportive, and action-focused.

- Coaching builds mindset, communication, confidence, emotional wellness, and follow-through
- Consulting strengthens strategy, systems, leadership, and team performance
- Each session ends with clear action steps

Progress is tracked and the plan evolves as needed.

- Measure wins and identify gaps
- Adjust strategy based on real-world results
- Reinforce habits, systems, and communication that support long-term success

### Choose Your Path

#### Coaching Path

Best for individuals, couples, and leaders who want personal or professional growth, stronger emotional wellness, and better daily execution.

#### Consulting Path

Best for business owners, professionals, and teams who need strategic clarity, process improvement, and better communication outcomes.

#### Combined Path

Best for clients whose goals involve both personal growth and organizational or leadership change.

### Logistics

Location: Sandpoint, Idaho (in person) and online  
Session length: 50 minutes (standard). 75 minutes available for couples or deeper work.  
Session frequency: Weekly or every other week. Most clients start weekly for 4 to 6 sessions, then shift to biweekly.  
Engagement options:  
- Free discovery call (15 to 20 minutes)  
- Single sessions (as needed)  
- 4-session Momentum Package  
- 8-session Growth Package  
- Monthly Maintenance (after initial progress)  
Pricing:  
- Individual coaching: $125 per 50-minute session  
- Couples coaching: $165 per 75-minute session  
- Consulting: $175 per 60-minute session  
- Packages: 5% to 10% savings when prepaid  
- Sliding fee scale: limited reduced-fee slots are available for local clients with financial hardship. Ask during the discovery call.
